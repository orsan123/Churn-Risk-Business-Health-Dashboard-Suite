# Churn Risk & Business Health Dashboard Suite | Power BI, MySQL, Python

Built to help 
GTM team monitor business health, customer engagement, support operations and detect churn early.

## Tools

- MySQL - data cleaning, transformation, feature engineering, query writing, and building layered reporting logic for scalable analytics
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib) - exploratory data analysis, churn behavior analysis, and visualization of customer engagement patterns
- Power BI (DAX, Data Modeling) - dashboard development, KPI creation, risk segmentation, and stakeholder reporting


## Business Context

This project was built for a corporate card and expense management company where the GTM team could use better visiblity into churn risk and overall business health.

The goal was to track:
- monthly business performance
- monthly active customer engagement
- early stage churn risk
- support operations

This helps both high-level executives and operational teams make faster data-driven decisions


## Objective

Can customer churn be spotted early?
## Key Insight

- Churned companies showed ~60% lower engagement compared to active companies in their first 60 days of their lifecycle.
- This validated **early lifecycle engagement** as a **strong signal for future churn.**

## Business Impact

This dashboard suite helps the company improve retention by giving both leadership and operational teams teh ability to track key metrics and make data informed decisions.

- **For leadership:** It provides a clear exectuive view of the overall business health.
